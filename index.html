FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

What is Udyam Registration and who is eligible for it?

Udyam Registration is a government registration scheme introduced by the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) for the classification and recognition of MSMEs. Any entity that falls under the definition of Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises can apply for Udyam Registration. The eligibility criteria for Udyam Registration are based on investment in plant and machinery or equipment and annual turnover.

What are the benefits of obtaining a GST registration and how can I register for it?

Goods and Services Tax (GST) registration is mandatory for any business whose turnover exceeds Rs. 20 lakhs. The benefits of obtaining GST registration include the ability to collect GST from customers, claim input tax credit on purchases, and access various government schemes. To register for GST, you need to visit the GST portal and submit the required documents.

What is FSSAI License and how can I apply for it?

FSSAI License is a mandatory requirement for any business that deals with food products in India. The Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) issues the license to ensure that the food products are safe for consumption. To apply for an FSSAI License, you need to visit the FSSAI portal and submit the required documents.

How can I determine if my business needs a Trademark registration and what are the benefits of obtaining one?

Trademark registration is important for any business that wants to protect its brand name or logo from infringement by others. If you have a unique name or logo that you want to use for your business, it is advisable to get it registered. The benefits of obtaining a trademark registration include exclusive rights to use the trademark, legal protection against infringement, and the ability to license or sell the trademark.

What are the consequences of non-compliance with the legal requirements related to Udyam Registration, GST Registration & Filling, FSSAI License, etc.?

Non-compliance with legal requirements related to Udyam Registration, GST Registration & Filling, FSSAI License, etc. can result in penalties, fines, and legal action. The government can also restrict your business operations and you may lose access to various government schemes. It is important to comply with all the legal requirements to avoid any negative consequences.
